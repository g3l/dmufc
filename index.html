DMUFC is the biggest sports society at De Montfort University. We have 6 11 a-side teams that compete in the BUCS league and Cup Competitions, with travel to away games against other universities across the country. We also have a 7th development squad that competes in the local intramural league.

I don't play football, Can I still join the club?

Yes, Everyone is welcome. We have a lot of lads that don't play but are just as much a part of the club as anyone else!
