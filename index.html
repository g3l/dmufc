DMUFC is the biggest sports society at De Montfort University. We have 6 teams that compete in the BUCS league and Cup Competitions on Wednesdays against other universities across the country. We also have a 7th development squad that competes in the local intramural league.

I don't play football, Can I still join the club?

YES!!! We have a lot of lads that don't even play but are just as much a part of the club as anyone else! With DMUFC, Wednesday is easily one of the best days of the week, if not the best! People have made friends for life here at the club. Joining DMUFC is something that you will not regret and will reveal a whole different uni experience!
