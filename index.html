DMUFC is the biggest sports society at De Montfort University. We have 6 teams that compete in the BUCS league and Cup Competitions on Wednesdays against other universities across the country. We also have a 7th development squad that competes in the local intramural league.

I don't play football, Can I still join the club?

YES!!! Everyone is welcome here. We have a lot of lads that don't even play but are just as much a part of the club as anyone else!
